I highly recommend Bridgette as a web consultant. Bridgette and I worked together to build and troubleshoot the SeeCurrents.com website for over two years. This was no simple task as the WordPress theme the site is built on offers extensive flexibility in many areas and significant restrictions in a few key areas. She was the consummate professional from the beginning. She helped me focus on the purpose and functionality of the site, created the wireframe, designed the site, ensured color and text consistency, created reusable templates, identified multiple plugins to make the site do what we intended, and spent many hours fixing numerous bugs. Another benefit of working with Bridgette was that, when necessary, we connected via face-to-face or web-based meetings. She is a great partner for freelance web development work.

Pat Davis, Owner SeeCurrents
